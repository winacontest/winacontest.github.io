Win A Contest is a Sweepstakes newsletter for residents of Canada containing information on where to win prizes by entering contests.

What it offers: Win A Contest Newsletter provides detailed information on provincial and national contests where you can win trips, cars, cash, and other valuable prizes.
What it covers: Mail In & In Store contests which Canadian residents are eligible to enter & win.
 Available through: Email or Canada Post
  Details offered
 - contest closing date
  - number of times you can enter
 - how often you can enter
- number of prizes
 - eligibility
- age restrictions
 - any comments about the rules
 - where to find the official form (if required)
- and where possible a hand drawn facsimile of the Universal Product Code
 Extras: Earn credits by sending us information about contests
  Our promise: We will continue to look for new contests and update this newsletter on a weekly or biweekly basis.
