The CanSweep Mailing List is a free place where Canadian sweepers can get together to share news about Canada-only Internet contests and sweepstakes. On the CanSweep list, members can also exchange tips, ideas, questions, and win announcements relating to Canadian contesting.

CanSweep also has a companion list now called AllSweep. Allsweep is for Internet contests that are Canadian-friendly (meaning contests that are also open to people in other countries)
You can sign up for AllSweep by visiting the following address: www.onelist.com/subscribe.cgi/allsweep

Note: If you want to receive e-mail's from both lists, you have to subscribe to both lists.
