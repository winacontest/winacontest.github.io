1. What is Win a Contest Newsletter?

Win a Contest is your one stop contest newsletter for all your contesting needs. On week by week basis it provides you with information on hundreds of new contests released all across Canada, including Mail-in, In-store, online, and from all types of magazines, newspapers, radio and television stations. You name it, we got it! Be prepared for the ultimate contesting experience.

2. Am I guaranteed to win big?

The newsletter provides information regarding various contests and sweepstakes offered by companies all over Canada. If you enter on a consistent basis, the probability of winning is in your favour. Perseverance and patience is the key to successful contesting.

4. I have subscribed to the Newsletter. What should I do if I do not receive it in my inbox?

If for some reason you do not receive all parts of the newsletter: please email winacontest.com@gmail.com with alternate email address and we will be happy to resend it. The alternate email address cannot be from the same ISP as the email address that rejected the newsletter. For example if you did not get it at shaw.ca, please do not send an alternate email address from shaw.ca It will not work. Please send from an alternate email address from gmail.com, Canada.com, yahoo.ca etc. (Not from Hotmail though since it is rejecting the newsletter more often than not). Gmail is a good alternate site for an email address. Just visit www.gmail.com and make one, and you will not have any more problems!!! Thanks for your cooperation and patience in this matter.
